Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, online banking, and mobile wallets.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to check the status of your delivery.

What is your return policy?

You can return products within 30 days of receiving them. Please ensure the items are in their original condition.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer shipping within Nepal. International shipping is not available at this time.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through the Contact Us page on our website.

What should I do if I receive a damaged product?

If you receive a damaged product, please contact us immediately through the Contact Us page with your order details and photos of the damage.

Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?

Order changes or cancellations can be made within 1 hour of placing the order. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes, our physical store is located at Basundhara, Kathmandu. You can visit us during our opening hours: 7AM - 7PM.
